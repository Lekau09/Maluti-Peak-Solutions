Statistical Analysis

Rigorous, clearly explained data analysis for academic research, surveys, and student projects — using SPSS, R, Python, and Excel.

What Is This Service?

Collecting data is only half the work. The other half is making sense of it — and that's where many students and researchers get stuck. Statistical analysis requires both technical knowledge and the ability to interpret results meaningfully.

We help you analyse your dataset, run the right statistical tests, produce clear visualisations, and present your findings in language your supervisor, lecturer, or audience will actually understand.

How This Helps You

  • Get accurate, reliable analysis of your research or survey data
  • Understand your results — not just receive numbers but know what they mean
  • Produce publication-ready charts, graphs, and tables for your report or dissertation
  • Save time on the technical side so you can focus on interpreting and writing
  • Use the right test for your data type — we advise on methodology, not just execution
  • Receive annotated output so you can explain the analysis in your own words

Tools We Use

SPSS

Ideal for social science research and survey analysis.

R

Powerful open-source tool for complex statistics and visualisation.

Python

Flexible and widely used for data science and analysis tasks.

Excel

Accessible and effective for straightforward datasets and charts.

Types of Analysis

  • Descriptive statistics (mean, median, mode, standard deviation, frequencies)
  • Correlation analysis (Pearson, Spearman)
  • Regression analysis (linear, multiple, logistic)
  • Hypothesis testing (t-tests, ANOVA, chi-square)
  • Data cleaning and preparation
  • Chart and graph production (bar charts, scatter plots, histograms, etc.)

Who Is This For?

  • University students completing dissertations or research projects
  • Researchers analysing survey or experimental data
  • Businesses analysing customer data or operational metrics
  • NGOs and organisations processing programme evaluation data
