Professional CV Design

A CV that does justice to your qualifications — clean, modern, and built to make recruiters stop and read.

What Is This Service?

Most CVs get less than 10 seconds of attention before a recruiter decides whether to keep reading. Design, layout, and how well your information is structured make all the difference in those first few seconds.

We design professional CVs and résumés that are visually polished, logically structured, and formatted to work with both human readers and Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) — the software many employers use to screen applications.

How This Helps You

  • Make a strong, immediate first impression with employers and recruiters
  • Have your experience and qualifications presented in the clearest possible way
  • Pass through automated ATS screening that filters many applications
  • Stand out from other candidates with a clean, modern design
  • Receive an editable file so you can update it yourself going forward
  • Get guidance on how to describe your experience effectively

What's Included

Custom Design

A layout tailored to your field, experience level, and target role.

Content Structuring

We help organise your information in the most impactful order.

ATS-Friendly Format

Formatted to pass automated screening software used by employers.

PDF & Word Files

Delivered in both formats — one to submit, one to edit later.

One Revision Round

We incorporate your feedback after the first draft.

Cover Letter Option

We can design a matching cover letter template on request.

Who Is This For?

  • University students preparing for internship and graduate applications
  • Recent graduates entering the job market for the first time
  • Professionals looking to update or modernise an outdated CV
  • Career changers moving into a new field or industry
  • Anyone who knows their current CV isn't doing them justice
