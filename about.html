Our Story

About Maluti Peak Solutions

A student-led consultancy built on the belief that digital opportunity should reach everyone — not just those who can afford premium prices.

How We Started

Our simple observation

We noticed something that many in Lesotho already know: talented people — students finishing degrees, entrepreneurs starting businesses, researchers completing studies — often lack access to the digital tools and professional services they need to move forward.

Hiring a web designer abroad is expensive. Getting a CV professionally designed costs money many students don't have. Statistical analysis support for a dissertation can be nearly impossible to find locally at a reasonable rate.

Maluti Peak Solutions was created to change that. We're a team of students and young professionals in Information Systems, Computer Science, and Statistics — putting our combined skills to work for our community.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and professional digital solutions that help students, entrepreneurs, and small businesses improve their online presence, professional image, and academic productivity.

Our Vision

To become a trusted and innovative digital consultancy recognised for empowering individuals and businesses through creative, reliable, and modern digital solutions.

Core Values

What Guides Our Work

These four principles aren't just words on a wall — they're the standards we hold ourselves to in every project.

Professionalism
