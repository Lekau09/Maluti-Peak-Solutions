FAQs

Common Questions

How much do your services cost?

We don't have fixed prices because every project is different. We'll give you a clear, honest quote after you tell us what you need — no surprises, no hidden fees.

How long does a project take?

It depends on the service and scope. A CV typically takes 2–3 days. A basic website takes 1–2 weeks. We'll give you a realistic timeline when we discuss your project.

Do you work with clients outside Lesotho?

Yes — all our services can be delivered remotely. We work via email, WhatsApp, and video calls, so location is not a barrier.

What do I need to provide to get started?

Just a description of what you need. We'll ask follow-up questions from there. For a website, we'll need your content and branding. For a CV, your experience history. For data analysis, your dataset and research questions.
